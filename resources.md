# Resources

A curated collection of the best Y Combinator resources for founders, aspiring entrepreneurs, and startup enthusiasts.

## Essential Reading

### Paul Graham's Essays

Paul Graham, YC co-founder, has written extensively about startups. These are must-reads:

#### Foundational Essays

- **[How to Start a Startup](http://www.paulgraham.com/start.html)** - The essential guide to starting a company
- **[Do Things That Don't Scale](http://www.paulgraham.com/ds.html)** - Why manual work matters in early stages
- **[Maker's Schedule, Manager's Schedule](http://www.paulgraham.com/makersschedule.html)** - Understanding different work styles
- **[Black Swan Farming](http://www.paulgraham.com/swan.html)** - YC's investment philosophy explained
- **[How to Get Startup Ideas](http://www.paulgraham.com/startupideas.html)** - Finding problems worth solving

#### Growth & Strategy

- **[Startup = Growth](http://www.paulgraham.com/growth.html)** - What defines a startup
- **[Default Alive or Default Dead?](http://www.paulgraham.com/aord.html)** - Understanding your runway
- **[The 18 Mistakes That Kill Startups](http://www.paulgraham.com/startupmistakes.html)**
- **[Mean People Fail](http://www.paulgraham.com/mean.html)** - Why being mean is bad for business

#### Team & Culture

- **[What We Look for in Founders](http://www.paulgraham.com/founders.html)**
- **[How to Be an Expert in a Changing World](http://www.paulgraham.com/ecw.html)**
- **[Keep Your Identity Small](http://www.paulgraham.com/identity.html)**
