## üí° Startup Wisdom

### Key Principles

1. **Make something people want** - YC's motto
2. **Talk to users** - Constantly get feedback
3. **Build quickly and iterate** - Ship fast, learn fast
4. **Focus on product-market fit** - Nothing else matters until you have it
5. **Do things that don't scale** - Manual processes are fine early on
6. **Choose co-founders carefully** - Co-founder conflicts kill startups

### Common Mistakes

- ‚ùå Not talking to users early enough
- ‚ùå Building for too long before launching
- ‚ùå Hiring too quickly
- ‚ùå Focusing on the wrong metrics
- ‚ùå Ignoring unit economics
- ‚ùå Raising too much money too early

## üéØ Application Tips

### The YC Application

- Why now?
- Why you and your team?

- Know your numbers cold
