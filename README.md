# Awesome Y Combinator [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated list of awesome resources, articles, books, and tools about Y Combinator and startups.

Y Combinator is a renowned startup accelerator that has helped launch over 4,000 companies including Airbnb, Dropbox, Stripe, and many more. This list contains valuable resources for founders, entrepreneurs, and anyone interested in the startup ecosystem.

## Official Resources

- [Y Combinator](https://www.ycombinator.com/) - Official Y Combinator website.
- [YC Startup Library](https://www.ycombinator.com/library) - Collection of essential advice for startups.
- [YC Blog](https://www.ycombinator.com/blog) - Latest news and insights from Y Combinator.
- [Hacker News](https://news.ycombinator.com/) - Social news website focusing on computer science and entrepreneurship.

## Books

- [The Lean Startup](http://theleanstartup.com/) by Eric Ries - Methodology for developing businesses and products.
- [Zero to One](https://www.zerotoonebook.com/) by Peter Thiel - Notes on startups and building the future.

## Articles & Essays

- [Paul Graham's Essays](http://www.paulgraham.com/articles.html) - Collection of essays on startups, programming, and life.
- [How to Start a Startup](http://startupclass.samaltman.com/) - Stanford course by Sam Altman and others.

## Videos & Talks

- [YC YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/ycombinator) - Videos featuring YC partners, founders, and startup advice.
- [Startup School](https://www.startupschool.org/) - Free online course for founders.

## Podcasts

- [Y Combinator Podcast](https://www.ycombinator.com/blog/category/podcast) - Conversations with successful founders and YC partners.

## Tools & Resources

- [YC Application](https://www.ycombinator.com/apply) - Apply to Y Combinator.
- [Bookface](https://bookface.ycombinator.com/) - Private social network for YC founders (YC alumni only).

## Success Stories

- [YC Companies](https://www.ycombinator.com/companies) - Directory of all YC-funded companies.
- [Top Companies](https://www.ycombinator.com/topcompanies) - YC's most successful companies by valuation.

## Community

- [Work at a Startup](https://www.workatastartup.com/) - Job board for YC companies.
