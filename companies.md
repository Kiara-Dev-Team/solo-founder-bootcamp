## Success Factors

What do successful YC companies have in common?

1. **Strong technical founders** - Deep expertise in their domain
2. **Clear vision** - Solving a real, painful problem
3. **Rapid iteration** - Quick to build and learn
4. **Focus on metrics** - Data-driven decision making
5. **Network effects** - Leveraging YC community and alumni
