## Events and Programs

### Demo Day

The culminating event of each YC batch:

**Format:**
- Each company presents for 2.5 minutes
- Audience of 500+ investors
- Two separate Demo Days per batch
- Virtual + in-person format

**Impact:**
- Average raise: $1-3M
- Meets 100+ investors in one day
- Media coverage and exposure
- Momentum for fundraising

**Open to:**
- Invited investors only
- Not open to public

## Hacker News

**[Hacker News](https://news.ycombinator.com/)**

YC's community forum and news aggregator:

**What it is:**
- Tech news and discussion
- "Hacker" = programmer/maker
- Voting-based ranking
- Text-focused, minimal design

**Popular Sections:**
- **Ask HN** - Ask the community anything
- **Show HN** - Share your projects
- **Jobs** - Job postings (first of every month)
- **Launch HN** - Official product launches

**Culture:**
- Intellectual discussion
- Thoughtful, substantive comments
- Strict community guidelines
- High signal-to-noise ratio

**Impact:**
- Front page = massive traffic
- Great for launching products
- Valuable feedback from technical audience
- Influential in tech community

**Tips for Using HN:**
- Read the [guidelines](https://news.ycombinator.com/newsguidelines.html)
- Be thoughtful and kind
- Focus on substance over style
- Respond to criticism constructively
